### 🧹 Resource Management

- Database resources (`Connection`, `PreparedStatement`, `ResultSet`) are not explicitly closed. This risks connection leaks.

### 🔍 Naming and Typographical Issues

- Business logic and persistence logic are mixed; consider separating data access logic into a DAO layer to improve single responsibility and testing.
